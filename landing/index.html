THE GAME

8-BIT ARCADE FEEL
Pixel-perfect Galaxian-style shooter. Drift, dodge, blast formations descending from the void. Tight 60 Hz fixed-step engine — every frame counts.
100 HAND-TUNED LEVELS
6 difficulty tiers from Tutorial to Legendary. Each tenth level is a boss. Levels unlock sequentially; first-clear gets a 2× score bonus.
USDC ON BASE
Pay for upgrades — extra lives, armor, rockets, wingmen — directly in USDC on Base. No tokens, no contracts, no rug pulls. Just a wallet transfer.
VERIFIED SCORES
Backend replays every run from your input stream. Cheaters can't fake a high score — the engine is deterministic given (seed, inputs).
WEEKLY LEADERBOARD
Compete for the top spot each week. Even runs that end in death count — every hit, every kill, every second adds to your lifetime POINTS.
DAILY MISSIONS
Three rotating challenges every day. Survive without taking a hit. Clear a boss in under a minute. Build streaks across days.
