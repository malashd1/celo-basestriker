USDC has de-pegged in the past and could again. The Base network is a Layer 2 rollup operated by Coinbase; outages, censorship, sequencer failures, or governance changes can prevent transactions from confirming. We are not responsible for any loss caused by USDC de-peg, network outage, gas price spikes, RPC unavailability, or any other infrastructure failure outside our control.

You do not grant us any allowance, you do not call any custom contract, and you do not lock up any funds. That said: bugs in your wallet, bugs in the underlying USDC contract, or bugs in Base infrastructure could affect transactions. We are not responsible for losses caused by such third-party bugs.
